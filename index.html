How to Enhance
Creative Thinking

如何增進創意思考能力

Using personal observation, imitation, and practice as key methods.
以個人進行觀察、模仿及練習等方式為例。

Observation

觀察

The ability to observe and understand the concrete or abstract thinking of others.
觀察及瞭解他人具體思考或抽象思考的能力。

Developing a keen eye for details that others might miss. It involves noticing subtle changes and nuances.
培養對細節的敏銳觀察力，發現他人忽略的細微之處。
Personal Experience

When reviewing a classmate's presentation, I noticed a font inconsistency that others missed. Pointing this out helped improve the overall visual flow.

在審查同學的簡報時，我注意到字體的不一致會分散注意力。指出這一點有助於改善整體的視覺流暢度。
Identifying the core essence or main problem within a complex situation. Focusing on what truly matters.
在複雜的情況中識別核心本質或主要問題，專注於關鍵所在。
Personal Experience

While reading a complex article, I practiced summarizing each paragraph into one sentence. This helped me grasp the main argument quickly.

在閱讀一篇複雜的文章時，我練習將每一段總結成一句話。這幫助我快速掌握主要論點。
Breaking down observed phenomena into smaller components to understand the logic and structure behind them.
將觀察到的現象分解為更小的部分，以理解其背後的邏輯與結構。
Personal Experience

To understand why a specific advertisement was popular, I analyzed its color scheme, music, and pacing separately to see how they worked together.

為了理解某個廣告為何受歡迎，我分別分析了它的配色、音樂和節奏，看看它們如何協同作用。

Imitation

模仿

The behavior of imitating the concrete or abstract thinking of others.
模仿他人具體思考或抽象思考的行為。

Adjusting the imitated idea to fit your specific context or personal style, rather than blindly copying.
調整模仿的想法以適應特定的情境或個人風格，而非盲目照抄。
Personal Experience

I liked the layout of a famous website, but instead of copying it directly, I changed the color palette to better match the style of my own project.

我喜歡某個知名網站的版面，但我沒有直接抄襲，而是更改了調色板以更符合我自己專案的風格。
Having a clear goal for why you are imitating specific behaviors. What outcome do you hope to achieve?
對於模仿特定行為有明確的目標。你希望達成什麼樣的結果？
Personal Experience

I practiced copying the writing style of a specific author with the specific target of learning how they used metaphors to describe emotions.

我練習模仿特定作者的寫作風格，目的是學習他們如何使用隱喻來描述情感。

Practice

練習

Constantly cultivating the habit of concrete and abstract thinking.
不斷培養自己的具體思考與抽象思考的習慣。

Building a mental library of ideas, patterns, and solutions that can be recalled and remixed later.
建立想法、模式和解決方案的心智庫，以便日後回憶和重新組合。
Personal Experience

I keep a "daily idea journal" where I write down interesting things I see. Reviewing this memory bank later helps me combine old ideas into new concepts.

我保留了一本"每日創意日記"，記下我看到的有趣事物。日後回顧這些記錄有助於我將舊想法組合成新概念。
Learning through doing. Accumulating practical knowledge from successes and failures over time.
從做中學。隨著時間積累從成功和失敗中獲得的實踐知識。
Personal Experience

I volunteered to organize a school event. The first time was chaotic, but that experience taught me better time management skills for the next time.

我自願組織了一場學校活動。第一次很混亂，但那次經驗教會了我更好的時間管理技巧，以便下次改進。
Testing your creative ideas in the real world to prove they work effectively and refining them based on results.
在現實世界中測試你的創意想法，證明其有效性並根據結果進行改進。
Personal Experience

I came up with a new study method. Instead of just assuming it was better, I verified it by comparing my test scores before and after using it.

我想出了一種新的學習方法。我沒有只假設它更好，而是通過比較使用前後的考試成績來驗證它。
