How to Enhance
Creative Thinking

如何增進創意思考能力

Using personal observation, imitation, and practice as key methods.
以個人進行觀察、模仿及練習等方式為例。

Observation

觀察

The ability to observe and understand the concrete or abstract thinking of others.
觀察及瞭解他人具體思考或抽象思考的能力。

Developing a keen eye for details that others might miss. It involves noticing subtle changes and nuances in your environment.
培養對細節的敏銳觀察力，發現他人忽略的細微之處。
Identifying the core essence or main problem within a complex situation. Focusing on what truly matters.
在複雜的情況中識別核心本質或主要問題，專注於關鍵所在。
Breaking down observed phenomena into smaller components to understand the logic and structure behind them.
將觀察到的現象分解為更小的部分，以理解其背後的邏輯與結構。

Imitation

模仿

The behavior of imitating the concrete or abstract thinking of others.
模仿他人具體思考或抽象思考的行為。

Adjusting the imitated idea to fit your specific context or personal style, rather than blindly copying.
調整模仿的想法以適應特定的情境或個人風格，而非盲目照抄。
Having a clear goal for why you are imitating specific behaviors. What outcome do you hope to achieve?
對於模仿特定行為有明確的目標。你希望達成什麼樣的結果？

Practice

練習

Constantly cultivating the habit of concrete and abstract thinking.
不斷培養自己的具體思考與抽象思考的習慣。

Building a mental library of ideas, patterns, and solutions that can be recalled and remixed later.
建立想法、模式和解決方案的心智庫，以便日後回憶和重新組合。
Learning through doing. Accumulating practical knowledge from successes and failures over time.
從做中學。隨著時間積累從成功和失敗中獲得的實踐知識。
Testing your creative ideas in the real world to prove they work effectively and refining them based on results.
在現實世界中測試你的創意想法，證明其有效性並根據結果進行改進。
